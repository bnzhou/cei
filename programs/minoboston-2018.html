What We Do

English Teacher (ESL)

To provide English teachers (ESL) in the US to expose a native educational environment (high school to kindergarten in Mino-city) and local community as workshop opportunities.


Read More

Internship Opportunity

To provide Japanese language students in the US to expose a native educational environment (high school to kindergarten as well as the general public in Mino-city) and the local community as an internship opportunity.


Read More

Summer Camp

To provide American high school and university students experience life as a member of a Japanese family, to speak and interact in Japanese and to learn about rural life, traditional arts, industries and culture in Japan.
