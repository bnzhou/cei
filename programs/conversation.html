CEI Online English-Japanese Conversation

We create online conversation sessions between native Japanese people and English speakers in the US. Polish your English communication skills or get familiar with fundamental Japanese conversations.

Sign Up Find My Group

Time:
Every weekend from May 9 through May 31. Each conversation session will be for one hour.

Sat, 9am, US Eastern Time (Sat, 10pm, Japan Time)
Sun, 9am, US Eastern Time (Sun, 10pm, Japan Time)


Japanese speakers and English speakers will be divided into small groups.
Each group will have around four participants.
At the beginning of each conversation session, there will be a 10-min self-introduction time.
Then, a 25-minute Japanese conversation practice followed by a 25-minute English conversation.
