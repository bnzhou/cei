CEI English-Japanese Workshop

We create weekly online workshops between native Japanese people and English speakers. Connect and help others with their language learnings - speaking, writing, reading, listening, and improving social and cultural understandings.

Sign Up Join Workshop
About the workshop

Every week, one hour workshop session.
Japanese speakers and English speakers will be divided into small groups.
At the beginning of each workshop session, there will be a 10-min self-introduction time.
To make the best use of this workshop, make sure to bring some materials to work with your partners.
