Interview

Eligibility Requirments

  • Open to anyone who is at least 19-year-olds
  • Have fundamental Japanese skills
  • Must be able to join and participate in the full length of the program

All applications will be evaluated based on

  • Your study abroad experience
  • Japanese skills
  • Your recommendations
  • How thoroughly you answer the essay questions outlined below
Essay questions
  • What are your goals for studying abroad?
  • What is the biggest challenge for you studying abroad?
  • Japanese culture is very different from American culture. How will you handle culture shock?
  • How can you contribute to the mission of cultural exchange by building mutual understanding between America and Japan?
  • How will you continue to study Japanese after the program?
  • Why should the program invest in you? What can you bring to the program?

Interview Information

We will have a one-on-one online interview with each selected candidate. The interview will be a 30-minute conversation with one of the CEI staff member.

In order to best evaluate and understand a candidate’s Japanese fluency level, a short Japanese conversation will be included in the interview. You will be asked and required to answer several questions in Japanese. At the end of the interview, you will have time to ask any questions that you may have.

Once you are selected for the interview, you will receive an email from us. After that, please book your interview time slot by clicking the following button.
